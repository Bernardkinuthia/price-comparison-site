2.1 Commission Structure

Here's how our affiliate relationship with Amazon works:

2.2 What Qualifies for Commissions

We may earn commissions on:

2.3 What We Don't Earn Commissions On

  • Gift cards and certain restricted categories

  • Returns, cancellations, or refunded items
